About MUSGAAU

The Muslim Graduates Alumni of Adekunle Ajasin University (MUSGAAU) serves as a unified platform for graduates of our prestigious institution to connect, support, and collaborate. Our mission is to promote the spiritual, social, and economic welfare of our members while giving back to our Alma Mater and the broader Ummah.

Our Core Objectives

  • Establish a unified digital registry for all Muslim graduates of Adekunle Ajasin University (Akungba-Akoko).
  • Provide a structured onboarding and verification system to preserve the integrity of our network.
  • Promote professional growth through job opportunities, career guidance, and mentoring.
  • Maintain clear, accountable, and transparent financial records and dues configurations.
  • Exchange knowledge, updates, and announcements in a respectful and structured environment.

Platform Administration

This web portal is administered by the MUSGAAU Executive Committee. Roles are separated into standard Members, Admins (responsible for registry approvals and announcements), and Super Admins (responsible for managing system configurations and admin privileges).
